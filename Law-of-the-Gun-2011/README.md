# 📜 Law of the Gun (2011 Original)

This folder archives the initial conceptualization phase of the "Bandits" era. 
This story was originally created as a practice comic without any intention of going further, 
however I fell in love with the characters and their world which lead this story to evolve into greater works.

### Technical Focus:
* **Initial Logic:** A study into using Paint Studio Sai and more Web Comic hard colors after coming from Photoshop and softer water color based brushes
* **Character Contrast:** Experimenting with greatly cartoonish exaggerated forms with original characters **Long Arm Jason** and **Few Words Robert**.
* **Narrative:** An twist of understanding for an arrogant bandit.
